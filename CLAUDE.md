## Active visual direction: Lumo Signal

When working on the storefront UI, follow the implementation brief in
`docs/lumo-signal-redesign.md`.

Visual references:

- `docs/design-references/lumo-signal-desktop.png`
- `docs/design-references/lumo-signal-mobile.png`

Treat these images as references for composition, hierarchy, color, typography,
spacing, card treatment, and responsive behavior. Do not copy titles, prices,
or other generated placeholder content from the images. Real project data and
existing catalog/business logic remain the source of truth.

The store now sells gift cards / top-ups (Steam, PlayStation, App Store, …)
sourced from NS.gifts — not games. The reference images and
`docs/lumo-signal-redesign.md` predate that pivot and still say "games"; read
their visual system (paper/ink/lime, budget-slider hero, card treatment) as the
source of truth for *look*, but the product concept is products/denominations,
not games.

The highest-priority surfaces are the home page, header, budget selector,
product rows, and product cards. Keep the rest of the current pages visually
consistent with the same design system.
